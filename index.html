Run Zombie Street

Welcome to Run Zombie Street, the game that will test your survival skills in a post-apocalyptic world filled with zombies! In this adventure game, you'll have to run, jump, and collect items while escaping from an endless horde of pursuing zombies. But don't worry, you can also defend yourself by throwing objects you find along the way. However, be careful not to rely too much on your running and jumping abilities. The path to freedom is filled with dangers and obstacles, such as crumbling buildings, abandoned cars, and narrow streets. If you're not paying attention, you could fall into deadly traps. In essence, Run Zombie Street is an exciting and challenging action game that will put your reflexes and survival skills to the test in a post-apocalyptic world full of zombies. So get ready for some action and become the hero of the apocalypse!.
